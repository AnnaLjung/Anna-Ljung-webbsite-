Anna Ljung is a Swedish artist and creative director whose work exists at the intersection of sculpture, textile art, spatial design, and poetic movement.

Known for her refined organic aesthetic and deeply tactile approach, she creates work where Scandinavian minimalism meets emotional depth. With decades of experience across interiors, craftsmanship, visual storytelling, and design — shaped between Stockholm and the Pacific coast of Costa Rica — she has worked as an interior designer, stylist, and featured talent in television productions focused on design and interiors.

Today, her practice is primarily centred around textile-based artworks, sculptural installations, and kinetic objects exploring the relationship between nature, light, movement, and atmosphere.

Working from her studio in Stockholm, Anna co-develops Kinetic Bloom — a contemporary sculptural practice where textile, form, and motion merge into immersive and poetic spatial experiences.
